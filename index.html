Welcome

Welcome to my CS 499 Computer Science Capstone ePortfolio. This portfolio presents the knowledge, technical abilities, and professional skills I developed throughout the Bachelor of Science in Computer Science program at Southern New Hampshire University.

The primary artifact featured in this portfolio is an Android-based Evidence Tracker application. The application originally began as an inventory management project created for CS 360: Mobile Architecture and Programming. I later customized the application to manage evidence records because the subject closely relates to my professional experience as a Crime Scene Investigator.

During the CS 499 capstone course, I reviewed and enhanced the application in the areas of software design and engineering, algorithms and data structures, and databases. These enhancements demonstrate my growth as a computer science student and my ability to design secure, maintainable, and user-focused software.
